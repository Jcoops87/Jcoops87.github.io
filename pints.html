why visit asheville

As part of the #CoopsinCarolina fall adventure, we spent 3 nights in Asheville, North Carolina. I knew Asheville only because for beer. That it supposedly had the most breweries per capita of any town in America. But I was pleasantly surprised by the walkable downtown, the crisp fall air, and the easy access to the mountains.
