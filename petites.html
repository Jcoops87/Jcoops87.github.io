petite sizing

who does it right

LOFT

Overall, I'm not the hugest fan of Ann Taylor LOFT's asethetic. But they absolutely nail petite sizing. So much so that the second I find a look I like, that is offered in petite, I buy it as quickly as possible. I've had great luck with their swing dresses and sweaters. The dresses are appropriate length and I especially love the sizing of the sweaters because the shoulder width is much smaller. Perfect for a narrow-shouldered petite bird like me!

banana republic

Banana Republic is a staple on the Petite scene, and offers a large variety of styles. I have to give it up for their pants which always seem to be about the right length and are great for work.
